PDF Conversion Tips

Get the most out of your document conversions with these professional tips:

  • Preserve formatting: Use standard fonts for better compatibility across devices
  • Image quality: Optimize images before conversion to balance quality and file size
  • Hyperlinks: Check that all links remain active after conversion
  • Bookmarks: Convert Word headings to PDF bookmarks for easy navigation
  • File size: Compress large files before emailing or uploading
